PRIVACY POLICY .

1. Purpose

This Privacy Policy explains how Zenith Pixels collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our website and mobile games.

2. Zenith Apps and Games

What data does Zenith collect?
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • In-game progress and achievements
  • Advertising IDs (IDFA, GAID)
  • IP address and approximate location

3. Data Sharing

We may share your data with third-party service providers for analytics, advertising, and cloud storage. These partners are required to protect your data and comply with relevant privacy laws.

4. Your Rights

Access & Portability

You have the right to request a copy of your personal data in a structured format.

Rectification & Erasure

You can request that we correct or delete your personal data at any time.

5. Children's Data

Zenith Pixels does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If we learn that we have collected such data, we will take steps to delete it immediately.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:zenithpixels.dxb@gmail.com
