The Zenith Pixels Story

WE ARE CRAFTING THE FUTURE
OF MOBILE PLAY.

Founded with a simple vision: to connect the world through happiness and serious play. Today, Zenith Pixels is a premier mobile game studio specializing in hyper-casual and puzzle games.

We are committed to pushing the boundaries of mobile entertainment by combining innovative mechanics with stunning visuals. Our goal is to create memorable experiences that players return to time and time again.

Through a culture of continuous learning and creative freedom, we empower our team to explore uncharted territories in gaming. We believe that by nurturing talent and embracing diverse perspectives, we can continue to surprise and delight players with every new release.
